AI Model Details
Three Models. Weekly Retraining.
Explainable to Every Worker.
🎯

XGBoost — Risk Pricing

Predicts Zone Risk Factor (0.8–2.5) from 5-year weather history, disruption frequency, seasonal patterns. SHAP values explain premium in plain language.

🛡️

Isolation Forest — Fraud

Anomaly detection on 7 signals. Genuine rider: long path (score 0.08, auto-approve). Spoofer: short path (score 0.92, auto-reject + appeal).

📊

LSTM — Forecasting

Time-series prediction of disruption probability per zone per day. Drives proactive premium adjustment and worker pre-alerts.

🔄 Model Lifecycle

  • Initial training on 5-yr historical + simulated claim data
  • Weekly retraining every Sunday midnight
  • Champion/Challenger: new model serves 5% shadow traffic
  • Auto-promote after beating champion for 2 weeks

📈 Drift Monitoring

  • Feature drift: input distribution shift > 15% → alert
  • Prediction drift: fraud score distribution change → alert
  • Concept drift: approval rate deviates > 10% → review
  • All tracked via MLflow + Prometheus dashboards

🕵️ Syndicate Detection

  • Registration clustering (>20 signups / 48hrs)
  • Device fingerprint overlap (IMEI, model, OS)
  • UPI payout graph analysis (50 workers → 5 UPIs)
  • Zone-level spike detection (Z-score > 3.0)
  • Adjacent zone comparison (400% spike vs. neighbors)

🏅 Trust Score System

  • New worker starts at 0.5 (neutral)
  • Each genuine claim: +0.05, appeal upheld: +0.10
  • Confirmed fraud: -0.15, spoofing app: -0.10
  • ≥ 0.8 → "Trusted Rider" badge, auto-Green
  • Transparency: clear language explaining every flag
